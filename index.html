Welcome to Team ICE Github

Information about our project

Our project is to create a customrom for the Xperia X8 that looks like Ice Cream Sandwich and has some of the Ice Cream Sandwich features bundled with it. The customrom is also gonna be fast and stable so its stable even for both users that play games on their phones and regular users that just make calls and listen music.
